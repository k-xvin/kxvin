# Phone Holder Replacement Part
I lost the little plastic piece that secures the phone holder to the car vent. The holder itself is some generic one I got off of Amazon.

I modeled and 3D printed out a new one that has a few extra "hooks" on it can stay attached without falling off again.

You can find the model on [Printables](https://www.printables.com/model/1485366-car-phone-holder-replacement-mounting-ring) or [locally here](/content/attachments/car_phone_holder_ring.zip).
